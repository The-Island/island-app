About

What's going on here?

Island is sort of a group blog with content cultivated by some of the world's best climbers. Our core Island Team is nearly 80 athletes strong.

In the latest release, we've shifted our focus to mapping in an effort to provide features that are useful to everyone. We're interested in real-time questions like, "Who's climbing where and what"? and "Where are the best climbing conditions"?, and on-going ones like "What's the best photo or video of this climb"?

It's all about the Island Map.

What we did -

We've built a map with most of the world's crags. The Island Team can use their Instagram service (#island) to post images from the field that will appear on the Island Map. This feature will soon be open to all members.

What we're doing -

Our nerds are in their caves working on linking past and future media to the Island Map, crag check-ins, ascent tracking, a top-secret climbing condition index, media ranking with voting, and world peace to boot.

We love maps. Stay tuned for our updates. Many thanks to our friends over at CartoDB (I'm looking at you Andrew W Hill).

Please send any questions or concerns to sander@island.io.
