About

What's going on here?

Island is a group media blog with content cultivated by some of the world's best climbers. Our core Team is nearly 80 athletes strong.

Recently added features -

The Island Map is new. Climbers can use their Instagram service (#island) to post images from the field that will appear on the Island Map.

What's coming -

... mapping existing media, crag check-ins, ascent tracking, a top-secret climbing condition index, and media ranking with voting.
